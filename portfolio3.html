Supervised Learning: I wanted to test a relationship between ATP Ranking and number of Winners hit by the Top 100 Male Tennis Players. Unsupervised Learning: I wanted to use Dimensionality Reduction through Principal Component Analysis to try an determine ATP Ranking in 2022. I used a key set of tennis playing metrics to determine, these metrics can be seen in the tooltip of this graph.

The outcome is that most of the top 10, 20 and 30 are well grouped together. Click the legend to indentify each ranking band.
